Plan Your Cultural Food Trip

Local Cuisine

"Dograma"the national dish, it consists of boiled mutton, onions, and freshly baked bread broken into pieces, all mixed together

Shashlik

Skewered and grilled meat—typically lamb—served with onions, often found as a staple street food.

Tips

Travel light. Bring cash. Respect the Country.
